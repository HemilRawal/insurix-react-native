# Insurix — Product Overview

Insurix is a mobile insurance management app built with React Native / Expo. It targets individual health insurance customers and allows them to:

- View and manage their active insurance policy (plan details, renewal dates, deductibles)
- Assess personal health risk via a multi-step onboarding questionnaire (BMI, lifestyle, pre-existing conditions)
- Browse and compare insurance plans with AI-driven recommendations based on their risk profile
- Track benefit usage and recent claims
- Interact with a built-in AI chatbot assistant (InsurX) for support and proactive renewal reminders

The app uses Firebase for authentication and data persistence. Currency is displayed in Indian Rupees (₹). The brand name is **Insurix** and the chatbot is referred to as **InsurX**.
